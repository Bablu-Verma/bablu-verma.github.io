Got Questions?

Commonly Asked
Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Typically, a web project takes 3 to 6 weeks, and mobile apps can take 6 to 10 weeks depending on the complexity, features, and integrations required.

How do you handle project communication?

I use tools like Slack/WhatsApp for quick chats, Jira/Trello for task tracking, and weekly video calls to ensure we're always aligned on the project goals.

Do you help with app store & cloud deployment?

Yes, I handle the entire lifecycle — from coding and testing to deploying on AWS, Vercel, Google Play Store, and Apple App Store.

What tech stack do you recommend for startups?

For most startups, I recommend the MERN (MongoDB, Express, React, Node) stack or Next.js. It's fast, scalable, and has a huge developer ecosystem for future growth.

How do you ensure post-launch support?

I provide 3 to 6 months of active maintenance support after the launch to ensure everything runs smoothly and to address any minor adjustments needed.

Do you offer custom designs or use templates?

I build every project from scratch using custom code. This ensures your website or app is unique, matches your brand identity perfectly, and performs significantly better than generic templates.

Is my website/app going to be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. In today's world, mobile-first design is a priority. Your project will be fully responsive and tested across all major devices and screen sizes to ensure a flawless user experience.

Can you help with SEO and Google ranking?

Yes, all my web projects follow SEO best practices, including fast loading speeds, clean semantic HTML, schema markup, and meta tag optimization to help you rank better on search engines.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I offer flexible maintenance and update plans. whether it's a small tweak or a major new feature, I'm available for long-term collaboration to keep your product evolving with your business.

Do you sign NDAs (Non-Disclosure Agreements)?

Yes, client confidentiality is a top priority. I am happy to sign an NDA before we discuss any sensitive project details or business strategies to ensure your intellectual property is protected.
