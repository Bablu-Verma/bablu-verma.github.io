I specialize in React, Next.js, Node.js, MongoDB, CSS, Git/GitHub, and AWS. I also work with APIs and integrate third-party services.

Do you provide UI/UX design as well?

Yes! I use Figma and Framer to create clean, modern, and user-friendly interfaces before development begins.

Minor revisions are included post-delivery. Major changes or new features will be billed additionally.
